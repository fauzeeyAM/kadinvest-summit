SUMMIT AGENDA

Five Days of Investment, Partnerships & Economic Dialogue

Day 1

Sunday, 14 June

Arrival of Participants

Delegates, investors, government officials, development partners and exhibitors arrive in Kaduna for registration, networking and pre-summit engagements.

Pre-Summit Activities

Sectoral exhibitions, strategic Business-to-Business (B2B) meetings and Government-to-Business (G2B) investment sessions focused on partnerships, opportunities and deal-making.

Day 2

Monday, 15 June

Day 3

Tuesday, 16 June

Official Opening & Gala Night

Official summit opening ceremony, keynote speeches, high-level investment sessions, policy dialogues and an evening gala celebrating partnerships and innovation.

Technical Sessions & Closing Ceremony

Deep-dive technical discussions across strategic sectors followed by summit resolutions, investment announcements and closing ceremony activities.

Day 4

Wednesday, 17 June

Day 5

Thursday, 18 June

Departure of Participants

Departure of delegates, investors, exhibitors and strategic partners following the conclusion of summit activities.
